About Trae.ai

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Trae.ai began with a simple mission: to help businesses navigate the complex digital landscape with innovative, AI-driven solutions.

What started as a small team of digital enthusiasts has grown into a full-service digital marketing agency with expertise across SEO, marketing automation, AI solutions, and web development.

Our journey has been defined by continuous learning, adaptation to emerging technologies, and an unwavering commitment to delivering measurable results for our clients.

Today, we're proud to have helped hundreds of businesses across various industries achieve their digital marketing goals and establish a strong online presence.
